Keto Bomb

Description

These 4-ingredient chocolate keto brownie bombs are dangerously delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup nut butter of choice, or allergy friendly
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 4-5 tbsp sweetener of choice, or as desired
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • mini chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Blend everything together in a food processor—scraping down the sides as needed—until it forms a smooth dough
  2. Roll into balls
    • If you've added melted chocolate or coconut oil, refrigerate just until they are firm enough
    • Scoop into balls with a mini cookie scoop
  3. be sure to report back if you experiment!
